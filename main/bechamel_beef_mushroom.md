### Ingredients of the Sauce:
- 1000g Milk
- 700g Minced Beef
- 3 Onions
- 200g Sauteed Chanterelle
- 2 Gloves of Garlic
- 1 Bush of Parsley
- 1 Chili
- 20g Apple Cider Vinegar
- 1 Stock cube
- Roux
- Salt & Pepper

### Method:
1. Make roux
2. Brown beef and sautee other incredients
3. Add parsley, vinegar and other stuff
