# Focaccia

### Ingredients:
- 770g Flour
- 630g Water
- 35g Sugar
- 15g salt
- 40g Olive Oil
- 7g Active Dry Yeast

### Method:
1. Combine everything
2. Let it rest in room temp for one hour
3. Overnight in the fridge
4. Oil sheet and put the dought in
5. Add toppings and oil to the top
6. Bake at 225 until golden brown
